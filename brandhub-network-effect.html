The Platform Network

The Flywheel

01 · Brand Growth
More brand clients join
Each new brand brings their existing vendor relationships and POS spend — no new vendor sourcing required to launch.
02 · Vendor Pull
More spend = more vendors want in
Platform spend volume attracts new vendors who pay to access the brand buyer network — growing the catalog and improving pricing for all brands.
03 · Select Leverage
Visibility drives production wins
brandhub shows Select's pricing and capability across every category. More brands on the platform means more production opportunities for Select to compete and win.
04 · Distributor Gravity
Scale unlocks distributor deals
As brand count grows, distributors gain access to a structured multi-brand POS program with pooled buying power — creating a new revenue layer tied to volume.
