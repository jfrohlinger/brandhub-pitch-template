Stream 3: Vendor Network
Vendors pay a % of the non-print, non-Select-produced spend that flows through their category on the brandhub platform. Vendors brought by existing brand clients are exempt — the fee only applies to spend flowing through brandhub's platform-sourced vendor network.

Assumptions

How it works: Vendors pay a % of the non-print, non-Select-produced spend that flows through brandhub in their category. Print is excluded. Spend Select wins in production is excluded from the vendor fee — that spend earns production margin instead (Stream 4). The vendor fee applies only to third-party vendor spend processed through the platform.

Stream 4: Select Production Margin
Select earns production revenue on categories it wins. This is the full top-line revenue from production — what brands pay Select to manufacture. Where Select both manages and produces a category, it earns production revenue only — no managed service fee on that same spend. Gross profit is shown separately for reference.

Assumptions

Production Categories: Print is Select's core production business. Displays, wearables, on-premise, and specialty items represent growth categories where brandhub visibility creates new production opportunities.
